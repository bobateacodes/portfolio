about me.

Temay Reyes Paguyo Broadway
Based in the Twin Cities, Minnesota

bachelor of science in three-areas
studio arts, interdisciplinary design, and computer science

I’m an artist and designer, and I love all things fun and interactive. As a multimedia designer, I blend my love for arts and digital media to visualize, communicate, and create engaging content and experiences. Whether it be through illustrations or photography along written stories, user-friendly and communicative designs, short videos or films, or combining games and interactive arts, I use multimedia to develop stories, inform, and entertain new experiences.

By using the knowledge I’ve learned through my passion for these three disciplines, I’m looking to work in organizations that want to inform, educate, and engage their audiences in captivating new ways that blend tradition and technology. I hope that I can encompass the things that I love while exploring creativity and collaboration with others.
